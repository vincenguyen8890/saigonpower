## Customers (Contacts)

The **Customers** page lists all contacts in the system.

### Adding a Customer
1. Click **Add Customer** (top right)
2. Fill in required fields: Name, Phone, Email, ZIP
3. Set Service Type, Status, and Source
4. Click **Save**

### Searching & Filtering
- Use the search bar to find by name, email, phone, or ZIP
- Use **Filters** to narrow by status or service type
- Tabs: **All Customers**, **My Customers** (assigned to you), **Unassigned**

### Viewing a Customer
Click a customer's name to open their detail page. You will see:
- Contact info (Phone 1, Phone 2, Email 1, Email 2)
- DOB and ANXH
- Tags (Assistant Program, 65+, Red Flag, VIP)
- Account Status
- Linked deals, activities, and notes

### Editing a Customer
On the customer detail page, click **Edit** to open the edit modal.

Key fields:
- **Phone 1 / Phone 2** — primary and secondary phone numbers
- **Email 1 / Email 2** — primary and secondary emails
- **DOB** — date of birth (MM/DD/YYYY)
- **ANXH** — account number or reference
- **Tags** — click to toggle: Assistant Program, 65+, Red Flag, VIP
- **Account Type** — Residential or Commercial
- **Account Status** — Active, Inactive, Switch Away

## Leads (Pipeline)

The **Leads** page shows contacts being actively worked in the sales pipeline.

- Filter by status: New, Contacted, Qualified, Proposal Sent, etc.
- Click a lead name to open their full profile
- Use **Quick Add** in the top bar to add a lead fast

## Deals & Opportunities

The **Deals** page lists all deals with their stage, value, and close date.

### Adding a Deal
1. Click **New Deal** (top right)
2. **Select a Contact first** — the Deal Title auto-fills as "FirstName – Service Address"
3. Fill in:
   - **Provider** and **Service Type**
   - **Contract Term** — select from dropdown (3, 6, 12, 18, 24, 36, 48, 60 months, or Month to Month)
   - **Contract Start Date** — Contract End Date calculates automatically (except Month to Month)
   - **ESI ID**, **Rate (¢/kWh)**, **Usage (kWh)**
   - **Flags** — click to toggle: TOS, TOAO, Deposit, Special Deal, 10% Promo
4. Click **Create Deal**

### Filtering Deals
Use the stage tabs at the top: All, Prospect, Qualified, Proposal, Negotiation, Won, Lost.

### Editing a Deal
Click **View** on any deal row to open the deal detail page, then edit fields inline.

## Accounts

The **Accounts** page shows all customers who have at least one deal on record.

- Filter by Account Status: **Active**, **Inactive**, **Switch Away**
- Search by name, email, or phone
- Click **View** to go to the customer's full profile
- Account Status can be updated from the customer edit modal

## Work Queue

Shows tasks and follow-ups that need attention. Use this to manage daily outreach and callbacks.

## Contracts & Renewals

### Contracts
View all active and upcoming contracts. Shows provider, term, start/end date, and ESI ID.

### Renewals
Tracks contracts coming up for renewal. Agents can create renewal deals directly from this page.

## Providers & Plans

- **Providers** — list of energy suppliers with commission rates
- **Plans** — specific rate plans offered by each provider

Admins manage these lists. Agents use them when creating deals.

---

## Reports & Accounting

- **Reports** — activity summary, deal volume, agent performance
- **Accounting** — commission tracking per deal and agent

## AI Manager

The AI Manager is an internal sales intelligence assistant. Use it to:
- Ask questions about your pipeline ("How many deals closed this month?")
- Get customer summaries
- Request follow-up recommendations

Type your question in the chat input and press Enter.

## Admin

### Users
Add, edit, or deactivate CRM users. Only accessible to Admins.

1. Go to **Admin → Users**
2. Click **Add User**
3. Set name, email, role (Admin or Agent), and password

### Settings
Configure company-level settings and preferences.

## Quick Reference — Deal Flags

| Flag         | Meaning                                      |
|--------------|----------------------------------------------|
| TOS          | Terms of Service signed                      |
| TOAO         | Transfer of Account Ownership required       |
| Deposit      | Deposit required from customer               |
| Special Deal | Non-standard pricing or terms                |
| 10% Promo    | Customer received 10% promotional rate       |

| Tag               | Meaning                                      |
|-------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Assistant Program | Customer enrolled in assistance program      |
| 65+               | Customer is 65 years or older                |
| Red Flag          | Flagged for attention or risk                |
| VIP               | High-value or priority customer              |
